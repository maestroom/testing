Teams workflow must be setup in the Administration module prior to using IS-A-TASK:

The definition of Workflow within IS-A-TASK is creating dynamic custom project workflows based on a series of tasks that the case manager defines during the new project process.  The series of tasks are displayed and organized by teams and services.

The structure of these task are organized and grouped by each oranization as each organization is mapped differently.  An organization may be comprised of  internal and/or external Teams, Team Services, and Team Service Tasks.  Since each organization is structured differently, it is important to map out your internal baseline workflow map prior to implementing the elements into IS-A-TASK. Inovitech may offer assistance in deriving at this internal workflow map under our professional service offering.

Internal/external Teams may be comprised of the following:

-Case Manager Team view projects by cases (default team) - Thier primary focus is to submit projects and manage case budgets (with an option to work on tasks)

-Operation Teams view projects by teams (one or more teams)- Their primary function is to receive projects, work on tasks, and report status of tasks.
